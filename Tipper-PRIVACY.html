Privacy Policy

Bill Tipping · Last updated: June 20, 2026

Bill Tipping ("the App") is a tip calculator. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. The short version: it doesn't collect any.

Information We Do Not Collect

Bill Tipping does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage data. Specifically, the App:

  • Does not require an account, login, or sign-up
  • Does not connect to the internet or any server
  • Does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies
  • Does not use any third-party software development kits (SDKs) or services
  • Does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, photos, or any other device permissions
  • Does not use cookies

How the App Works

All calculations (bill amount, tip percentage, and total) are performed entirely on your device, in memory. Any values you enter exist only while the App is open and are discarded when you close or reset the App. Nothing you type is saved to disk, synced to iCloud, or sent anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Because Bill Tipping does not collect any information from any user, it does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 or any other age.

Third-Party Services

Bill Tipping does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or other services.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is ever updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.
