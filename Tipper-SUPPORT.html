Bill Tipping

Bill Tipping is a simple tip calculator: enter a bill amount, pick or adjust a tip percentage, and see the tip and total update instantly. This page covers how to use the App and how to reach us if something isn't working as expected.

How to Use Bill Tipping

  • Bill Amount — Tap the bill amount field and type in the amount from your check.
  • Tip Percentage — Tap one of the preset buttons (18%, 20%, 22%, 25%) or drag the slider to choose any percentage between 10% and 40%.
  • Total — The tip and total update automatically as you change the bill amount or tip percentage.
  • Edit the Total Directly — You can also tap the total amount and type in the total you want to pay (for example, to round up to an even number). Bill Tipping will work backward to figure out the matching tip and percentage.
  • Clear — While the keyboard is open, tap "Clear" to reset everything and start over.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the tip percentage change when I edit the total?
When you type a value directly into the total field, Bill Tipping calculates the tip percentage needed to reach that total from your bill amount, and moves the slider to match.

What's the maximum/minimum tip percentage?
The slider supports tip percentages from 10% to 40%. Editing the total directly will also be capped within this range.

Does Bill Tipping save my bill history?
No. Bill Tipping does not save, sync, or share any of the numbers you enter. Everything resets when you close the App. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does Bill Tipping require an internet connection?
No. Bill Tipping works entirely offline.
